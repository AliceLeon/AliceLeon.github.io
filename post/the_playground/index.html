The Playground

A few interactive programs for children for Pacee Lab.

Hopefully, they should be inspiring and fun to play with. The last two projects support up to 6 people to play at the same time.

A Pitch Game.

Singing in different pitch to move the light ball up and down; when hitting the glowing notes, a Minion-like sound will sing with you in corresponding pitch. #Unity

Hello You

A simple ball game. You can grab the balls and they’ll be watching you. In a certain rage, the more balls that gather, the deeper their colours will be. #Processing
